Who We Are

About Catalyst Gen AI

Catalyst Gen AI, founded in 2024, is the official tech society of the AIML & AIDS Department at JIMS Engineering Management Technical Campus. The club was created with a mission to ignite curiosity, inspire innovation, and empower students to explore the ever-evolving world of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and emerging technologies. Through hands-on workshops, technical projects, hackathons, research initiatives, and peer-learning activities, Catalyst Gen AI provides a collaborative platform where ideas turn into impactful solutions. We aim to build a community of passionate learners and creators who are ready to shape the future of technology with knowledge, creativity, and purpose.

Vision

To become a leading student-driven community that nurtures innovation, builds real-world AI solutions, and inspires the next generation of technologists to create meaningful impact in society.

Mission

  • Promote learning and hands-on exploration in AI, ML, Data Science, and modern technologies
  • Organize workshops, seminars, hackathons, and collaborative projects
  • Encourage research, experimentation, and innovation
  • Connect students with industry experts
  • Build teamwork, creativity, leadership, and technical excellence
